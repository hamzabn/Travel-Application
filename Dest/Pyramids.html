Pyramids of Giza

Map

Perhaps the most iconic of Egypt's ancient sights, the Pyramids of Giza are located just outside Cairo. Comprising three different pyramid complexes, the site is one of the original Seven Wonders of the World. Khufu's pyramid (also known as the Great Pyramid of Giza) is the largest and dates back some 4,500 years

it is both the oldest and the only one that still survives today. The three main pyramids serve as the tombs of the pharaohs Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure.

In front of the pyramids lies the Great Sphinx
